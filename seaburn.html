Coronavirus Update!

We are now back open!

Following the relaxing of government lockdown conditions on Monday 17th May 2021 our Amusement Arcades are now open.

We are still asking all customers to follow government guidance by wearing a face mask at all times, maintaining social distance, hand sanatising frequently and completing track & trace.

Guidance is changing regularly for our industry so please follow us on Facebook to keep up with any changes.

Fun for the whole family...

Seldons Leisureworld has been offering a fun-fuelled family day out on Seaburn seafront for over 40 years. With over 750sqm. of floor space packed with over 100 entertainment machines, we have something for everyone!

Ticket Machines

Win tickets by playing on our 2p pushers, basketball, skill games and many more.

Kiddie Rides

Bring your little one along for a ride on the teacups or a trip on the pirate ship.

Videos

Race your friends, venture through Jurrasic Park or visit a new virtual reality world.

Prizes, prizes & more prizes...

Win your tickets...

Test your skills across a huge range of machines and win as many tickets as you can. The more you win, the better the prize!

Save them up...

If you don’t collect enough the first time, don’t worry, simply feed your tickets into the ticket eater, and retain the ticket receipt until your next visit.

Visit the prize bar...

Visit the prize bar and select the awesome prize of your choice from our vast array on display. Don’t forget, the more tickets you collect, the bigger the prize you win!
